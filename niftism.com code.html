Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

Everything foreign founders ask before forming their Florida LLC

Can I form a Florida LLC if I don't live in the US?+
Yes. Florida has no residency requirement for LLC owners. You can be from any country. The only requirements are a Florida Registered Agent (Niftism provides this) and a principal office address in Florida (included in all plans).
How long does Florida LLC approval take?+
Florida's Sunbiz.org processes LLC filings in 1–2 business days. Once approved, you receive your Certificate of Organization by email. EIN from the IRS typically takes 1–5 additional business days.
What is the $125 Florida state fee?+
$125 is Florida's official filing fee for Articles of Organization, including a $25 Registered Agent designation fee charged by the Florida Division of Corporations. This is included in all Niftism plans — no hidden extras.
Will my Florida LLC get me approved on Stripe and Amazon?+
Yes. A Florida LLC with a valid EIN is fully accepted by Stripe, Amazon Seller Central, Shopify Payments, and PayPal Business. Our setup guides walk you through each platform's verification process.
What is BOI / FinCEN filing?+
The Beneficial Ownership Information (BOI) report is a federal requirement for most new LLCs. Penalties for non-filing can reach $500/day. Included in Scale and Dominate plans, and $49 add-on in Launch.
Do I need an ITIN or SSN to get an EIN?+
No. Foreign nationals can obtain an EIN using a passport or national ID number — no ITIN or SSN needed. Niftism handles this for you, included in all plans.
What languages do you support?+
We support English, Arabic, French, Spanish, and Portuguese. Support calls and email are available in all five languages. Select your language at the top of the page.
